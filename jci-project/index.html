We Are Creative And Awesome

The JCI Monastir was created in 1977 by the founding president Fredj RHAIEM. Since then, it has contributed with all the components of civil society to act at the local, regional and national levels in order to exploit the capacities of young Monastir executives in voluntary work for the well-being of the individual and the community

OUR MISSION

Provide development opportunities for youth by giving them the ability to create positive change.

For individuals

The personal development of our members is at the heart of our action. Within the JCI, everyone has the opportunity to learn, to discover new skills and above all, everyone has the right to make mistakes. We want our members to become better people and better citizens.

For the internationalism

through international conferences, exchange programmes, twinning between young chambers, JCI offers global development opportunities, international meetings and the strength of a united and supportive global community.

For the business

We are active in the heart of business communities and allow our members to take advantage of them to develop their business experience by learning through action, and to network with successful entrepreneurs. In this way, JCI members can learn and develop by participating in various projects to master the roles of managers, visionary leaders and coaches.

For the community

The goal is to provide opportunities to develop social responsibility and have a major role in creating positive change through civic projects within our community of life.
